Stefan Krasić was born on 15th of February 1992 in Niš, Serbia. He started studying film directing at the Academy of Arts in Belgrade in 2012 under the mentorship of a famous documentary director professor Dragoljub (Dragan) Elčić. Stefan is one of the three directors chosen in 2015 to create a film during an International film camp called Interakcija.
The promotional video for the Academy of Arts in Belgrade which he had written and directed was selected as the best directed promotional video in 2016 by the National Organization for Market Communications of Serbia. He is a founder of an independent production called Prinčevi Patroni which has been producing original works since 2009 and has made over 100 works of art related to film and television (commercials, tv series, music videos, sketches, comedy shows). His films Mosaic, Adornment and In Memory Of participated in numerous film festivals across the world.
The most significant awards are: The Award for the Best Documentary Short Film at the 63rd Belgrade Film Festival in 2016; the Golden Camera Award for the Best Documentary Film at 2016 VIVA Festival in Sarajevo; a Special Award called Golden Butterfly in Sarajevo in 2017; Golden Buklia for Best Directing in Velika Plana in 2015; The Award for the Best Student of the Academy of Arts in Belgrade. Mosaic is one of the films made by Stefan that stands out due to numerous public projections and discussions. He composed the music and wrote the lyrics for a band called Prinčevi Patroni.
