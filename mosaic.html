Mosaic (2014)

Documentary short film

Mosaic

29 min, documentary, FullHD, 2014

About: My grandma is constantly complaining about the fact that she doesn't have anyone to vacuum her house, change the curtains, and wash the dishes. Most importantly, she complains about being lonely. I want to understand her, knowing that maybe one day I will be lonely too. Yet, I fail to understand her complaining completely – she has someone by her side every day, and she is not alone at all.
